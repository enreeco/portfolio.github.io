Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

TabDeck is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information to provide you with the best experience:

  • Extension settings and preferences
  • Workspace configurations and tab organization data
  • Browser tab information (URLs, titles, and metadata)
  • Extension usage statistics for performance improvement

What We Don't Collect

We do NOT collect:

  • Personal identification information (names, emails, addresses)
  • Financial information
  • Sensitive browsing data or web page content
  • Passwords or authentication credentials

Data Storage & Security

All your data is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit your data to external servers, ensuring your information remains completely private and under your control.
