Your Privacy Matters

AIDA is designed with privacy as a core principle. We believe you should have complete control over your data and how it's used.

What We Collect

  • Extension Settings: Your preferences and configuration settings (stored locally only)
  • User Input: Text and prompts you enter when using AI features (stored locally only)
  • API Keys: Your AI service API keys (encrypted and stored locally only)

What We DON'T Collect

  • Personal identifiable information (names, emails, addresses)
  • Browsing history or web activity
  • Personal files or documents
  • Location data or device information

Data Storage & Security

  • Local Storage Only: All data is stored locally in your browser's IndexedDB
  • No Cloud Sync: We don't store any data on external servers
  • Your Control: You can delete all data at any time through the extension

Third-Party Services

When you use AI features, your content is sent directly to your chosen AI provider (OpenAI, Anthropic, or Google). We don't proxy, store, or access this data.

Key Point: Since all your data remains within your extension, you have complete privacy by default. We cannot access, retrieve, or recover any information stored in your extension.
